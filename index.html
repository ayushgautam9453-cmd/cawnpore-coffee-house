Our Story

A Cup Brewed With
Cawnpore's Old Soul

Long before it was Kanpur, this city was Cawnpore — a place of bustling bazaars, colonial bungalows and quiet courtyards. We named our house after that memory. Tucked inside Goel Apartment on Juhi Kalan Road, Cawnpore Coffee House is where the aroma of slow‑roasted beans meets the warmth of home‑style plates.

Every cup is pulled with intention. Every plate leaves the kitchen the way we'd serve it to family. This isn't a chain café — it's a neighbourhood ritual.

Head Chef & Culinary Curator
